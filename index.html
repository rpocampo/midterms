Starbucks

Starbucks is a global coffeehouse chain known for its specialty coffee drinks, cozy café atmosphere, and commitment to sustainability. Founded in 1971 in Seattle, Washington, it has grown to thousands of locations worldwide. Starbucks offers a variety of beverages, including espresso-based drinks, teas, and seasonal specialties, along with pastries and light snacks.

The brand emphasizes quality sourcing, often working directly with coffee farmers through its Coffee and Farmer Equity (C.A.F.E.) Practices. Starbucks is also recognized for its community engagement and efforts in environmental stewardship, including initiatives for recycling and reducing waste.

The iconic green mermaid logo and the customizable drink options have made it a cultural phenomenon, with many customers enjoying the experience as much as the drinks themselves. Whether you’re looking for a quick caffeine fix or a comfortable spot to work or socialize, Starbucks has become a popular go-to destination.
